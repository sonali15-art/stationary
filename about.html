Stationary at your door step

Led by a passion for innovative and entrancing stationery, the idea of ‘Stationary Land’ sparked in 2016. We realised that while India is filled with stationery enthusiasts, only a few knew about the plethora of creative yet practical stationery, art and craft supplies available. We travelled to various global stationery expos across Japan, Dubai and Germany handpicking some of the most innovative stationery brands.
